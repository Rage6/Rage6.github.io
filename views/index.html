RULES
- Each player has 8 rounds. After the second player's 8th round, the winner is the player with the highest total score.
- A player may roll the dice no more than three times during their round.
- Each category can only be used by each player ONCE. Available categories are shown in black on each player's scoreboard, while used categories are shown in red.
- Each category can only accept certain dice before being used.
Aces - Sixes: Only the dice with the same numbers as that of the category. (Ex. "Fives" = 5, 5, 5)
Chance: All dice must be selected, no matter what their numbers are. (Ex. "Chance" = 2, 5, 4, 4, 3)
Yahtzee: Only used if all five dice are the same number. (Ex. 2, 2, 2, 2, 2)
- The sum of the selected dice will be added to the player's total score... EXCEPT for a Yahtzee. A Yahtzee is always scored as 50, no matter which dice were used.
- It can happen for none of a player's dice to work with any available categories. In a case like this, the player must choose any available category, unselect all of their dice, and click 'Enter Points'. That category will now be considered 'used', even though its score will be 0.
HOW TO PLAY
1) ROLL THE DICE - To roll the dice, click the "Roll" button. Each of the dice will randomly generate a new number between 1 and 6.
2) CHOOSING DICE - If you double click on a dice and its color is then shades grey, then that dice has been "selected". A selected dice WILL NOT be rolled if the Roll button is used again. Any "unselect" (white-colored) dice WILL be changed during the next roll. Selected/unselected dice can be changed back simply by double clicking them again.
3) CHOOSE THE CATEGORY - Based on the dice that you have selected, click the category that you want to enter your points into. To see the rules of each category, click the 'Rules' button.
4) ENTER YOUR POINTS - Once your dice and category are chosen, click the 'Enter Points' button. The new category and total scores will be displayed in the player's scoreboard.
5) MOVE TURN TO NEXT PLAYER - The current player's name will be highlighted in their scorecard. Once you have entered your points, your turn is over. Simply click 'Next Turn' (which will now highlight their name) and then the next player can begin.
WHO WINS? - The winner is the player with the highest overall score after the final player enters their points on the 8th round.
DID AN UNEXPECTED BOX APPEAR? - This is likely because a dice, category, or action was selected at the inapporpriate time. Please read the explanation on the box, and you can probably find the mistake.
